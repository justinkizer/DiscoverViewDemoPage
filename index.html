Use the map

DiscoverView accesses your phone's GPS to locate your position and show you recent Instagram photos taken nearby. You can also drop pins on the map to see photos from those locations.

Get inspired

Scroll through photos to get a sense of an area.
Click on any photo to make it bigger and display a button to see where that photo was taken on the map. By long pressing a full screen photo, you can even save it to your camera roll or view it directly on Instagram.
